# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the BengoBox Notifications project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behaviour include:

- Showing empathy and kindness to others
- Being respectful of differing opinions
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour include:

- Sexualised language or imagery; unwelcome advances
- Trolling, insulting comments, personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers clarify and enforce standards, taking action against unacceptable behaviour.

## Enforcement

Report issues to [security@bengobox.com](mailto:security@bengobox.com). Complaints will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

1. **Correction** – Private communication clarifying expectations
2. **Warning** – Official warning with consequences for continued behaviour
3. **Temporary Ban** – Temporary removal from community spaces
4. **Permanent Ban** – Permanent removal for repeated/severe violations
